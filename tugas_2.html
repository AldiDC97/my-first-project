WOODAUM +82

Woodaum+82 is a restaurant specialized in Korean Premium Aged-Beef who maximizes the unique Korean beef flavor by using ​Woodaum’s special aging technology on high quality beef.

From Korea to Indonesia

Located in Yongsan-Korea, Woodaum+82 now brings ​authentic Korean cuisine to Indonesia for you to enjoy effortlessly.​
